  • MultiplayerSet is a game about pattern matching. The idea is to find sets of 3 cards that meet certain requirements. Each card has a variety of shapes on it. The properties that can vary are number (of shapes), shape, color, and shading.

    A set is 3 cards for which each card has, for each property, 3 different properties or 1 uniform property. Two cards that are both red and a third that is green are not a set.
